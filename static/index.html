China’s Water Crisis
Water health and safety is the biggest challenge to China’s sustainable growth. Over 50% of China’s water sources are contaminated. Nowhere is this strain felt more than China’s countryside. Over 298 million people in rural areas lack access to clean drinking water, a crisis which stunts economic growth and development.
Your Project
Learn and make a difference. You’ll work in cross-cultural teams to lead a needs-assessment of water quality challenges in selected villages in Yunnan. Guided by experienced educators and practitioners, your data-driven work will lay the groundwork for a water filtration NGO’s expansion into this region.
